About Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games, released in 2020. The game features 5v5 matches where players choose from unique "agents," each with special abilities, promoting strategic teamwork and precise gunplay.

Teams compete to plant or defuse a bomb (the Spike) on intricately designed maps, emphasizing communication and coordination. With its competitive esports scene, regular updates, and engaging art style, Valorant has quickly become a favorite among players, combining mechanical skill with strategic depth for an exciting gameplay experience.
